QuickWiki

themes

Themes are what controls how your QuickWiki looks and what the final html files will be structured like. You can download new themes and start using them by just changing a configuration variable. They are also easy to customize and develop on your own.

How to configure theme?

In your config.toml, you can set the value theme.name to whatever theme you want to use. Example to use the default theme:

[theme]
  name = "simpleblue"

How themes are loaded

When compilation and writing of posts happen, theme is loaded based on the name variable in the configuration. It tries to find ./themes/:name and load style.css and template.html from there. At that point magic variables are being replaced. Probably move these to golang templates in the future.
