Welcome to the official wiki of QuickWiki

QuickWiki is a simple wiki, driven by a simple binary that you run yourself and generates a static website of it. Together with markdown, themes and plugins, QuickWiki offers a fast, easy and extensible way of running your own, personal wiki.

Features

  • Generates static HTML-pages
  • All assets static
  • Wikis works offline of published over IPFS

Why have you own Wiki?

Having your own wiki (in general, no matter what platform) is useful for many things.

  • Knowledge
    • Remembering and writing down what you’ve learned about stuff
  • Dairy
    • One new page for each day, attach data and you got your own fancy diary
  • Software Documentation
    • Wikis are the best way of having your documentation!
  • General websites
    • QuickWiki can easily replace any normal website, by providing powerful plugins and data-keeping
